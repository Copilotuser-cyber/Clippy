Introduction to AI

Hello! Can you explain what artificial intelligence is?
Of course! Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. These machines can perform tasks such as recognizing speech, making decisions, solving problems, and more. AI can be categorized as narrow AI, which is designed for specific tasks, or general AI, which would have the ability to perform any intellectual task that a human can do.
That's interesting. What are some real-world applications of AI?
AI has numerous applications across various industries. Some common examples include:

• Virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa
• Recommendation systems on Netflix and Amazon
• Self-driving cars
• Fraud detection in banking
• Medical diagnosis systems
• Language translation services
